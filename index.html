Hello World! Welcome to my journey into web development. I have an academic background in humanities, with a particular interest in oral history. I have a passion for learning and I am currently spending much of my free time on freeCodeCamp (with some help from Lynda.com and Codecademy), working towards web developper certificates.

I have already completed Free Code Camp's front-end development certificate and I am halfway through their data visualization certificate. I am most comfortable with javascript and React, but I am also experienced with Bootstrap, Html5, CSS3 and much more. Most of my journey has been done through project-based learning and I would like to use this portfolio to share some of my favorite projects so far.

In my quest to better my skills, I have met many other passionate learners through FreeCodeCamp Montreal (for which I am a co-administrator) and Les Pitonneux. The support of other coders has been invaluable and I cannot wait to see where new skills and new friends will take me.
